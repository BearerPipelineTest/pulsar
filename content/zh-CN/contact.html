Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

The current process for reporting vulnerabilities is outlined here: Apache Security. When reporting a vulnerability to security@apache.org, you can copy your email to private@pulsar.apache.org to send your report to the Apache Pulsar Project Management Committee. This is a private mailing list.
